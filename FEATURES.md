## üéØ Matching Criteria

The platform considers multiple factors for matching:

1. **Mental Health Concerns**: Specific issues the person is dealing with
2. **Therapy Preferences**: Preferred therapeutic approaches
3. **Location**: Geographic proximity or remote preference
4. **Language**: Communication language preference
5. **Insurance**: Accepted insurance providers
6. **Demographics**: Gender preference, age group expertise
7. **Cultural/Religious**: Jewish community affiliation
8. **Session Format**: Individual vs. couples/family therapy
9. **Availability**: In-person vs. remote sessions
10. **Budget**: Price range considerations
